How it works

What is joya ?

Joya is a platform that simplifies life with diabetes and lowers the burden of diabetes monitoring. Joya helps you fix new health goals and accompanies you in every step of the way . Because we know that adopting a healthier lifestyle is complicated , we simplify everything by telling you what to do everyday, step by step ; We remind you to do what you have to do and always provide educative material so you can be conscience about every aspect of Diabetese .

How we can help you ?

Our features include Customized workouts to encourage you stay active and exercise safely, Meal plans so you can know what you should eat in order to keep your glucose levels stabilized, Professional coaching because everybody needs help every now and then and our coaches are always there to answer you questions and motivate you, Psychological support which includes everything from self development courses to feel good Yoga lessons !

Levels are assigned to each workout based on the movements being performed and the intensity of those movements.

On what basis do we suggest workout videos ?

In order for us to provide the most suitable workout videos, we ask you at the start of the program about your fitness level, your healthy condition and if you have any special conditions that we have to be aware of. Our suggestions change also according to your glucose level entry, your moods and your energy level so we always make sure to provide the best videos for you.

Why do I enter in my food ?

Nutrition is a key aspect to overall diabetes management, and by entering your food it allows you to be aware of how certain foods might be effecting your blood sugar levels. By entering your food, it will give you and your healthcare provider a chance to see patterns with food choice and blood sugar levels. Sharing this information with us will give us a fuller picture of how your diabetes management is progressing.

What happens with my personal data ?

Your personal information is securely saved in the cloud and cannot be shared with anyone unless you allow it.
